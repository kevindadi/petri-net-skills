# PTPN Lane

Use this lane for ordinary workflow or resource nets.

## Best Fit

- business processes,
- production flows,
- synchronization and mutual exclusion,
- sequential and branching state changes.

## Model Output

- place list,
- transition list,
- arc list,
- initial marking,
- plain-language intent note.

## Keep in Mind

- choose the smallest meaningful net,
- do not add token classes unless needed,
- do not overfit the structure to a specific implementation detail.
